## Step 2: Login

1. **Go to:** https://myra-status-dashboard.vercel.app/support/login

   - Email: (the one your admin gave you)
   - Password: (the temporary password)

---

## Step 3: Change Your Password (Recommended)

After logging in:

1. Go to your profile settings
2. Change to a strong password you'll remember
3. Done!

---

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid login credentials" error
- Double-check you typed the email and password correctly
- Passwords are case-sensitive
- Make sure there are no extra spaces

### Still can't login?
Contact your admin - they can reset your password instantly.
