## 🤔 What Is This Thing?

myRA AI is our shiny new dashboard for managing trial organizations. Think of it as your mission control for:
- **Tracking all your assigned trial orgs** (yes, you can finally stop using that Excel sheet from 2019)
- **Logging activities & notes** (with timestamps, because we're professionals now)
- **Managing trial timelines** (14-day trials that feel like 14 years)
- **Creating tickets** (for when trials go sideways, as they do)
- **Viewing reports** (pretty charts that make us look like we know what we're doing)

The best part? You'll ONLY see the orgs assigned to you. No more scrolling through everyone else's trials pretending to look busy.
